# The Vault

> High-quality, evergreen information on an AI-powered collector marketplace focusing on transparent pricing, AI appraisals, buyer matching, and secure transactions for collectibles.

The Vault is an online platform designed to connect collectors and buyers through a transparent, AI-driven marketplace. It offers tools for appraising items, listing for sale, and finding suitable buyers efficiently.
